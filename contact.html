After You Submit

What Happens Next

We keep the process simple and respect your time. Here's what to expect after you send a bid request.

We Review Your Project

Within one business day, your request lands with our estimating team. We review the scope, location, and timeline — and flag any questions before we reach out. No canned auto-replies.

We Connect With You

We'll call or email to confirm the details and answer any questions you have about our process. If you have plans or specs, mention them in your bid request — we'll follow up directly.

You Get a Real Bid

We deliver a clear, itemized bid with scope, schedule assumptions, and a direct line to your estimator. No inflated numbers to negotiate down — we price the job right the first time.

Prefer to talk first?

Call us directly. Our team is available Monday through Friday, 7 AM to 5 PM.
