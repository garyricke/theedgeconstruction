About This Project

This Northbrook health club required a complete interior build-out from structural framing through finish — locker rooms, spa areas, pool surrounds, fitness floors, and premium finishes throughout. The project demanded high-humidity-tolerant assemblies around wet areas alongside standard commercial framing in dry zones.

Our team delivered moisture-resistant drywall assemblies in all wet areas, standard gypsum partitions throughout the main fitness floor, and millwork elements in the lobby and locker room spaces. The finished facility reflects the kind of quality that keeps members coming back — and keeps the GC calling us for the next one.
