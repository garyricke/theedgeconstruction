## Description

The Edge Construction Co. is a commercial carpentry subcontractor serving the greater Chicago metropolitan area since 1996. The company self-performs five core scopes under a single subcontract: metal stud framing, wood framing, commercial drywall, commercial millwork installation, and specialty acoustic ceiling installations. Primary clients are general contractors on commercial, multi-family, retail, healthcare, hospitality, and mixed-use projects.

## Services

- Metal Stud Framing: Structural and interior light-gauge steel framing, complex assemblies, curved walls, tall-wall systems, curtain wall backup. Precision laser layout on every project.
- Wood Framing: Platform framing and engineered lumber systems for low-rise commercial and multi-family construction. Hybrid wood-and-metal scopes self-performed under a single subcontract.
- Drywall Solutions: Full commercial drywall scope — hanging, taping, finishing, Level 5. In-house prefabrication shop reduces on-site labor time by up to 75%. UL-listed fire-rated assemblies.
- Commercial Millwork: Precision installation of architect-specified casework, cabinetry, reception desks, wall paneling, and custom architectural woodwork. AWI quality standards.
- Acoustic Installations: Specialty ceiling clouds, wood slat systems, metal panel ceilings, geometric and honeycomb systems, acoustic baffles, and fabric-wrapped panels.

## Differentiators

- In-house drywall prefabrication shop (up to 75% faster on-site production)
- Procore project management on every project (submittals, RFIs, daily logs)
- Precision laser layout on all framing scopes
- Self-performs all five scopes — single subcontract eliminates trade coordination gaps
- 30+ years of Chicago-area commercial construction experience

## Service Area

Greater Chicago metropolitan area including: City of Chicago, North Shore, western suburbs (Bartlett, Elgin, Naperville, Schaumburg), southern suburbs, and broader Chicagoland across Cook, DuPage, and Kane counties.
