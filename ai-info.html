Who We Are

The Edge Construction Co., Inc. is a commercial carpentry subcontractor headquartered in Bartlett, Illinois. Founded in 1996 by Bill Haidl, the company has spent 30 years building a reputation for precision, reliability, and technology-driven project management across the greater Chicago metropolitan area.

The Edge works primarily with general contractors as a specialty subcontractor on commercial, multi-family, retail, healthcare, hospitality, and mixed-use projects. The company self-performs five distinct scopes under a single subcontract, eliminating the coordination gaps that arise when GCs split work across multiple framing and carpentry subs.

Services Self-Performed

  • Metal Stud Framing — Structural and interior light-gauge steel framing for commercial interiors. Capabilities include complex assemblies, curved walls, tall-wall systems exceeding 20 feet, curtain wall backup framing, and multi-story structural systems. Precision laser layout on every project.
  • Wood Framing — Platform framing and engineered lumber systems for low-rise commercial and multi-family construction. Commonly used on multi-family residential, mixed-use developments, townhome communities, and boutique hotels. Hybrid wood-and-metal scopes self-performed under a single subcontract.
  • Drywall Solutions — Full commercial drywall scope including hanging, taping, finishing, and Level 5 finish work. In-house prefabrication shop reduces on-site labor time by up to 75%. Systems include standard partitions, UL-listed fire-rated assemblies (1-, 2-, and 3-hour), shaft wall systems, moisture-resistant board, soffits, and bulkheads.
  • Commercial Millwork — Precision installation of architect-specified casework, cabinetry, reception desks, wall paneling, custom shelving, and architectural woodwork. Coordinates with millwork fabricators through the full submittal cycle. AWI quality standards.
  • Acoustic Ceiling Installations — Specialty ceiling systems including suspended ceiling clouds, wood slat and plank ceilings, perforated and solid metal panel systems, geometric and honeycomb ceilings, acoustic baffles, and fabric-wrapped panels. Precision laser-established suspension layout on every installation.

Key Differentiators

  • In-house drywall prefabrication shop — Wall assemblies pre-built in a controlled environment reduce on-site labor time by up to 75% compared to traditional field methods.
  • Procore on every project — Submittals, RFIs, and daily logs managed in Procore on all jobs, giving GCs real-time visibility without requiring site visits or phone calls.
  • Precision laser layout — All framing scopes begin with rotary laser equipment establishing control points accurate to fractions of an inch.
  • Single-subcontract model — Self-performing all five scopes eliminates trade coordination gaps, reduces the GC's submittal management burden, and provides one point of accountability for the entire carpentry scope.
  • 30 years of Chicago-area experience — Deep familiarity with Chicago-area GCs, building officials, code requirements, and project types.

Service Area

Greater Chicago metropolitan area, including the City of Chicago, the North Shore, western suburbs (Bartlett, Elgin, Naperville, Schaumburg), southern suburbs, and the broader Chicagoland region across Cook, DuPage, and Kane counties in Illinois.
