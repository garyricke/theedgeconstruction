About The Edge

Commercial carpentry contractors serving the greater Chicago area since 1996. One team. Four scopes. Full accountability.

Our Story

Built From
The Ground Up.
Still Building.

The Edge Construction Co. was founded in 1996 with a straightforward premise: commercial carpentry deserved a higher standard. What started as a focused framing crew serving the greater Chicago market has grown into a full-service commercial carpentry contractor trusted by GCs and developers across Chicagoland.

We operate out of Bartlett, IL, and our scope spans metal stud framing, drywall solutions, commercial millwork, and acoustic installations. Bringing those scopes under one roof — with one project manager, one point of contact, and one accountability structure — is what separates us from the typical sub stack.

Over 20 years, we've built in historic theaters, high-rise residential towers, healthcare facilities, auto dealerships, health clubs, and everything in between. The projects change. The standard doesn't.

What We Stand For

Four Principles.
Every Project.

These aren't aspirational statements on a wall. They're the standards we hold every crew, every foreman, and every project manager to — on every job, every day.

01

Safety First

Every job starts with a safety plan. OSHA-certified crews, daily toolbox talks, and a zero-incident mindset aren't policies — they're how we work. We go home the same way we showed up.

02

Precision Counts

We use laser layout on every project and run Procore from day one so every trade has the same information at the same time. Tolerances matter — not just for our scope, but for every trade that follows us. Sloppy framing costs everyone.

03

Technology Driven

We were early adopters of digital layout and in-shop prefabrication. Our approach reduces field waste, compresses schedules, and delivers repeatable results at a higher quality threshold than traditional methods.

04

Full Accountability

When The Edge is on a job, there's one number to call. We coordinate across all our scopes internally so GCs don't have to manage the gaps between trades. One team, one answer.

How We Work

One Contractor.
No Gaps.

Pre-Construction Partnership

We get involved early — reviewing drawings, flagging conflicts, and building scope packages that reflect what's actually in the field. Early coordination saves time and money for everyone downstream.

In-Shop Prefabrication

Our Bartlett shop pre-builds assemblies before they ever hit the jobsite — header packages, framed panels, millwork components. Less cutting in the field means faster installs and a cleaner site.

Laser Layout on Every Job

Every project is laid out with professional rotary laser equipment. No chalk lines, no guessing. Our layout is accurate, documented, and ready for the next trade to build off confidently.

Single-Point Coordination

Framing, drywall, millwork, acoustics — one project manager, one schedule, one contact. We resolve scope overlaps internally so they never become your problem on the job site.

Company History

Two Decades of Growth

1996

Founded in Chicagoland

The Edge Construction Co. opens its doors as a commercial framing specialist, building a reputation for precision layout and on-time performance across the Chicago metro area.

2008

Expanded Drywall Scope

Added commercial drywall installation and finishing to our service offering — allowing GCs to consolidate their framing and drywall under a single contract and schedule.

2012

Prefabrication Shop Opens

Launched our in-house prefab operation in Bartlett. Pre-building assemblies off-site before field installation cut labor hours, reduced waste, and enabled tighter project schedules.

2016

Millwork & Acoustics Added

Incorporated commercial millwork installation and acoustic ceiling systems, completing the four-scope model. We could now take a commercial interior from structural framing through finished ceiling — one team.

2020

Procore Adopted Company-Wide

Standardized on Procore for project management across every job — submittals, RFIs, daily logs, and schedule tracking all run through one platform, keeping GCs and owners informed in real time.

Today

500+ Projects Completed

With 20+ years of experience and 500+ completed projects across healthcare, hospitality, multi-family, retail, and historic renovation — The Edge continues to raise the standard for commercial carpentry in Illinois.
