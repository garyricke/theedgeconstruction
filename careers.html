About the Role

We're looking for experienced commercial metal stud framers who take precision seriously. You'll be working on a variety of projects across Chicagoland — from multi-family residential to retail build-outs to complex historic renovations — with a crew that knows what they're doing and takes pride in it.

We use laser layout on every job. If you've worked with rotary lasers and understand what tight tolerances mean for the trades coming after you, you'll fit right in.

What We're Looking For

  • 3+ years commercial metal stud framing experience
  • Proficiency reading architectural and structural drawings
  • Experience with laser layout equipment
  • Ability to work from scaffolding and aerial lifts (OSHA 10 preferred)
  • Reliable transportation to job sites across Chicagoland
  • Strong work ethic and attention to detail
  • Experience with curtain wall backup, tall-wall, or complex assemblies a plus

About the Role

The Edge operates an in-house prefabrication shop and invests in advanced finishing equipment — meaning our tapers work smarter, not just harder. We're looking for experienced commercial tapers and finishers who can deliver consistent Level 5 finish quality across a range of project types.

You'll work alongside our framing crews in a coordinated sequence, with project management tools that keep the schedule tight and the handoffs clean.

What We're Looking For

  • 3+ years commercial taping and finishing experience
  • Level 4 and Level 5 finish capability
  • Experience with spray texture and flat finish applications
  • Familiarity with automatic taping tools a strong plus
  • Ability to read and work from finish specifications
  • Reliable transportation to job sites across Chicagoland
  • OSHA 10 preferred; willing to train the right candidate

About the Role

We're looking for experienced commercial drywall hangers to join our production crews. Our prefab shop pre-builds many assemblies before they hit the field — so you spend your time hanging, not cutting and fitting from scratch. Efficient, clean, and professional.

Projects range from large multi-family residential to commercial tenant improvements across the Chicagoland area.

What We're Looking For

  • 2+ years commercial drywall hanging experience
  • Proficiency with lift equipment and working at height
  • Knowledge of fire-rated assembly requirements
  • Ability to read architectural plans and mark-ups
  • Efficient production pace on repetitive unit layouts
  • Reliable transportation to Chicagoland job sites
  • OSHA 10 a plus; will assist with certification

About the Role

We promote from within and we're growing. If you're an experienced framer who has run a crew — or is ready to — we want to talk. Foremen at The Edge are responsible for daily crew management, quality control, layout execution, and coordination with the GC and other trades.

You'll have access to our project management software, laser equipment, and direct support from our project managers. We set our foremen up to succeed — not just put out fires.

Apply for This Role →

What We're Looking For

  • 5+ years commercial framing experience
  • Prior foreman or lead carpenter experience preferred
  • Strong laser layout skills — runner and column lines, elevations
  • Ability to manage 4–10 person crews across a job site
  • Comfortable with project management software and daily reporting
  • Strong communication with GC supers and project managers
  • OSHA 30 preferred; valid driver's license required
