The Edge's Approach to Commercial Wood Framing

When a GC puts a wood framing scope with The Edge, they get the same rigor that drives our metal stud work: accurate layout from day one, daily schedule tracking, and Procore on every project so nothing falls through the cracks. We've been executing wood framing on Chicago-area commercial and multi-family projects for 30 years — enough experience to handle complex assemblies and the systems to keep your schedule moving.

We handle wood framing for multi-family residential, mixed-use developments, boutique hotels, retail buildouts, and light commercial construction throughout the greater Chicago area. Our crews are experienced with platform framing, engineered lumber systems, and hybrid projects that combine wood and metal stud framing under a single subcontract — giving GCs one point of contact and zero coordination gaps between framing trades.

How We Work

How Does The Edge Approach Every Wood Framing Project?

From pre-bid coordination to final inspection, every step is deliberate.

Pre-Bid Review

We review structural drawings, specifications, and sequencing requirements early — flagging scope conflicts and coordination issues before bidding. Procore is stood up from day one so nothing falls through the cracks.

Layout & Mobilization

Accurate layout establishes bearing walls, partition lines, and floor assembly references — the foundation every downstream trade depends on. Materials are staged and crews are briefed before mobilizing.

Framing Execution

Experienced crews execute the framing scope with tight daily tracking against schedule. Complex assemblies, engineered lumber elements, and hybrid metal/wood zones are pre-planned before crews mobilize.

QC & Handoff

Completed framing is inspected against approved drawings before handoff to drywall, MEP, and finish trades — reducing RFIs and field changes downstream.
