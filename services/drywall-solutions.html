What Commercial Drywall Services Does The Edge Offer?

Drywall is one of the highest-volume scopes on any commercial project — and the one most likely to run over on labor hours if the system behind it isn't optimized. At The Edge, we've invested in a dedicated prefabrication shop and state-of-the-art finishing equipment so that speed and quality aren't a tradeoff.

Our prefab shop pre-builds wall assemblies off-site in a controlled environment, then ships them ready to install. Combined with our advanced finishing systems, we deliver consistent Level 5 quality at production speeds that keep your schedule ahead of the curve — not chasing it. Fire-rated assemblies are installed per IBC fire and smoke requirements using UL-listed assembly designs.

By Bill Haidl, Owner & Principal — The Edge Construction Co., Inc.

The Prefab Advantage

How Does Prefabrication Speed Up Commercial Drywall Installation?

Traditional drywall installation happens entirely in the field — measuring, cutting, fitting, and adjusting each piece under job-site conditions. Our prefab shop flips that model. Assemblies are pre-built in a controlled environment by dedicated shop workers, then delivered ready to hang. Field crews spend their time installing, not cutting. Less waste. Less rework. Dramatically faster.

The result: projects like our 132-unit Lincoln Park apartment building were completed with on-site fabrication that kept the schedule weeks ahead of traditional methods — without a single unit failing inspection.
