Commercial Millwork

Two decades of precision millwork installation across retail, hospitality, office, and multi-family projects throughout greater Chicago.

What Makes Commercial Millwork Installation Different?

Millwork defines how a space feels — from the reception desk that makes a first impression to the custom shelving that carries a brand's identity. Poor installation undermines even the most beautifully fabricated product. That's why GCs across Chicago trust The Edge to be the last hands on their most visible work.

We coordinate from shop drawing review through final adjustment and punch list, ensuring every piece seats perfectly, every joint is tight, and every installation meets both the architect's intent and the owner's expectations. All millwork fabrication and installation adheres to AWI (Architectural Woodwork Institute) quality standards.

By Bill Haidl, Owner & Principal — The Edge Construction Co., Inc.

Common Questions

Commercial Millwork FAQ

Commercial millwork refers to custom wood and composite products — casework, cabinetry, wall paneling, reception desks, custom shelving, storefronts, and architectural woodwork — fabricated off-site and installed by a skilled contractor. It's one of the most visible scopes in any commercial interior.

We install millwork across retail storefronts, hotel lobbies and guest rooms, corporate offices, restaurants, auto dealerships, healthcare facilities, and multi-family residential buildings. With 20+ years in the market, we've worked in virtually every commercial setting in greater Chicago.

The Edge specializes in installation — we work with the architect's specified fabricator or the GC's selected millwork shop. Our value is in coordinating the shop drawing review, ensuring conditions are ready on-site, and executing the installation with precision that protects the fabricator's product and the owner's investment.

We have extensive experience in phased and partially occupied projects, including multi-family buildings with staged unit completion and occupied retail centers. We coordinate access windows, protect in-place finishes, and work to condensed schedules without cutting corners on quality.
