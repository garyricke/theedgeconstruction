Do Use Edge Blue for primary CTAs, links, and icons on light backgrounds. Use Edge Gold for accents, borders, dividers, and headlines on dark backgrounds.
Don't Never place Edge Gold text on white backgrounds (fails WCAG AA contrast). Never use Edge Blue on dark navy — use white or gold instead.
Note The Dark Scale is the primary background for hero sections, nav, and footer. Gold on dark passes WCAG AA at all heading sizes.
